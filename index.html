About Us

Wisdom HR was started with the sole objective of mentoring and nurturing Indian youth, transforming and making the unemployable into employable, guiding and placing them appropriately, taking on challenges via collective wisdom and overcoming such hurdles to the benefit of all concerned.

Recruitment.

In many ways, acquiring and accessing talent is among a company’s most critical goals. Without critical talent and skills, companies cannot grow their businesses. Yet in today’s new environment, the old ways of recruiting, acquiring, and accessing talent are no longer effective.

We at Wisdom help corporates big and small to acquire right talent and facilitate organizations enhance and enrich human assets.

Consultancy.

Wisdom is a corporate hub for human resource re-engineering and development. Customized training and development of the workforce are a part of our HR services. The boom in outsourcing human resource and recruitment solution renders opportunity for optimizing resources.

Training & Mentoring

Wisdom plays an integral part with partner organizations in transforming the workforce ambiance through expert training. Campus placement training leads to a good career start and our dedicated training wing provides training solutions to meet the complex challenges of the emerging work world. The training modules include certifications on career skills, induction program.

Professional training and development, empower employees with sharpened business skills and knowledge vital to meet workplace demands, eventually resulting in creating an efficient team of personnel and increased productivity for organizations. Our best training pedagogy ensures quality and progression to achieve greater success as a holistic development at an afforable cost.
